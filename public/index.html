Biography

I’m an assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of California, Riverside.

I’m an applied mathematician specializing in nonlinear dynamics and machine learning, with research spanning biology, cardiology, and ecology. I am particularly interested in tipping points and the development of computational tools to predict and understand them (TEDx talk).
